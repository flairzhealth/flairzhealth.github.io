EARLY INTERVENTION

1. Assess worker MSK + EH condition

Subjective / Objective assessments of the worker’s MSK conditions are done quarterly via AI tools deployed on mobile devices and on-premise kiosks. “Screening” and “corrective exercises” to support new or current employees working safely. Program is assigned algorithmically to specifically address location/employee need Additionally, AI health bots monitors worker’s heart rate at rest, sleep and anxiety levels as needed.

2. Monitor job specific stressors in real-time

Job specific stresses and repetitive movement on the body can be constantly monitored via wearables and remote devices. MSK injury risks based on certain frequency of moves are calculated in real-time and safety alerts are triggered when injuries are predicted. Wearables can track heart rate, heat and hydration levels at work.

3. Predict injury risks and recommend prevention programs

Specific “stretch and flex” MSK exercise programs are assigned by location through our digital platform In certain cases a “Wellness Program" is assigned algorithmically to address specific employee needs. Emerging risk patterns for the population is made available to the plant and safety managers via dashboards.
