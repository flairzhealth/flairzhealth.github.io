REWARDS AND CHALLENGES

utilizing rewards and incentives to motivate employees to exercise

How do we help?

Incentivizing participants to take an active step towards their health can come in different forms. Some people prefer to be motivated by a treat – something they can work towards to get a reward. We collaborate with different vendors that provide gift cards to many different stores. Other people prefer to be motivated by competition with their fellow employees. We offer a company wide ‘Challenge’ that incentives employees to collectively achieve the goal based on their location. Depending on the demographic, we offer different solutions.
