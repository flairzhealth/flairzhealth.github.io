HEALTH COACH

Flexible workflows for manage incidents and claims

How does it help?

When someone is putting an active effort towards their health, it can be difficult to talk about struggles and find the motivation to continue. Our health coach driven programs give each person on their health journey a space to discuss what they are having troubles with and provide insightful tips on staying motivated.

How we help?

Coaches are able to utilize our platform to build the information and exercises the employees will utilize in their sessions. Coaches are able to hold the participants accountable by reaching out to them via the Flairz platform and directly ask the participant why they are not following through. The personalization provided by Flairz allows coaches to have a hands-on experience with the participant's health journey, and allows for a tailored health program specific to the participants needs.
